Neuroloft handles all billing inquiries for customers across North America and Europe. Payments are processed through our system and customers can reach out to the support team if they experience any issues. They will review the case and take appropriate action. The billing department works with multiple vendors to ensure timely processing of all transactions. If there are any issues with a payment they should contact us immediately. Our team is available during business hours and they handle everything from refunds to payment disputes. The platform supports credit cards, wire transfers and digital wallets. For urgent matters reach out to them directly. All disputes must be submitted within 30 days. Refunds are typically processed within 5 business days from the date of the approved request. Make sure to have your account number ready when you call. The team also manages subscription renewals and cancellations. Submit your request via CRM and it will be routed to the appropriate team. Check the SLA for response time commitments. The PM needs to approve any exceptions to the standard refund policy.

Refund Process

To request a refund follow these steps:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Billing > Transaction History
  3. Click "Request Refund" on the relevant transaction

INTERNAL NOTE: The refund approval threshold was raised to $500 in Q3. Do not share this with customers. Agents should use their discretion for amounts between $200-$500.

Refunds are processed within 10 business days after approval. For amounts over $1,000 additional verification may be required. Contact the finance team for large refund requests.
